Teaching Philosophy

I believe all students are naturally curious. I believe in guiding students to be seekers of knowledge by showing them how to use tools to find answers to their questions and solutions to their problems. I believe in encouraging scholars to take charge of their own education, become life long learners and innovative digital citizens.
